# Ask AI Better
A simple prompt builder for students. Fill in your topic, task, level, and output format — then copy a clear prompt to paste into ChatGPT, Claude, or any AI helper.

## How it works
1. Enter your **topic**
2. Tap a **task** (explain, quiz, outline, etc.)
3. Choose your **level** and **output format**
4. Tap **Copy prompt** and paste into your AI chat
Yellow highlights in the preview show what’s still missing. Tap a highlight to jump to that field.

## Tips for students
- Be specific with your topic
- Ask for one thing at a time
- Double-check facts for assignments — AI can make mistakes
